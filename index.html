In a nutshell, the liberal market economy is for us just the accepted universal method of free trade of goods and services to which the goal of "wealth" creation is central. In the free market (that exists all around us and in every interaction with fellow men, official or private) wealth is found in what all parties to a successful trade are left with (wealth is the profit created after a win-win situation).

Innovation is a term whose circulation speed is exponentially increasing in our contemporary times, where there are as many definitions of the term as there are types of beverages on the market. We understand innovation as an improvement of the current state of affairs where the current state of affairs has a more important role than the improvement. An innovation should not namely be a revolution and thus mean a destruction of the current state of affairs, but rather an evolution, a gradual change. Everything functional and beneficial from the current condition should be detected and preserved with high regard until new improvements can safely be incorporated into it.



Our innovation for consulting, as an established sphere in every healthy market economy, consists of a premise that the functional should be maintained, for only in this way advice can lead to the adoption and the execution of the necessary activities that can bring concrete benefits to companies and their clients.
